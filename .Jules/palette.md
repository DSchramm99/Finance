## 2025-05-15 - [Financial Dashboard Visualization]
**Learning:** Standardizing financial data with `st.column_config` (Progress bars for scores, currency formatting) and `st.metric` significantly improves readability and professional feel compared to raw dataframes or custom HTML.
**Action:** Always prefer native Streamlit `column_config` for numeric scales and `st.metric` for high-level KPIs to ensure consistent styling and better accessibility.
